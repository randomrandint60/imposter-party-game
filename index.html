📖 How to Play

🕵️ Classic Mode

  • Everyone receives the same secret word (e.g. "Pizza").
  • The Imposter gets nothing — they must bluff!
  • Players take turns giving one-word clues. Then vote out the suspected Imposter.
  • If caught, the Imposter gets one final guess at the word to steal the win!

💬 Find the Liar Mode

  • Everyone receives the same question — except the Liar who gets a different but similar one.
  • All players answer their question aloud. The group votes out who they think answered differently.
  • Find the Liar before they blend in!

🏆 Win Conditions

  • Civilians win by voting out all imposters.
  • Imposter wins if they equal or outnumber civilians — or guess the secret word!
