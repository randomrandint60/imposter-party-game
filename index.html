How to Play

🎮 Setup

  • Add 3 to 12 player names.
  • Choose how many Imposters and Undercovers you want in the settings.
  • Select a category word pack.

🍕 Classic Mode

  • Everyone receives the same secret word (e.g., "Pizza").
  • The Imposter receives nothing — they must bluff their way through!

💬 Find the Liar Mode

  • Everyone receives the same question, except one player (the Liar) who gets a different but similar question.
  • For example: "Favorite summer food?" vs. "Favorite winter food?".

🗣️ Clue & Discuss Phase

  • Each player gives a one-sentence clue (in Classic) or answer (in Liar) based on their card.
  • Civilians must be clever: give a clue that other Civilians understand, but vague enough so the Imposter doesn't guess the word.
  • Imposters must bluff, guess the topic, and blend in!

🗳️ Vote & Win

  • Discuss who is lying and vote to eliminate a player.
  • Voted-out players reveal their card.
  • If the Imposter is eliminated, they get a final chance to guess the Civilians' word to steal the win!
